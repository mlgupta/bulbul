Manage your creativity and experience in Printoon. My Printoon provide a facility to Register as new customer to become Printoonite, at the same time facility to manage profile for existing Printoonites. To know More facilites that My Printoon gives you, see below.

Register: Are you New to Printoon? You can register as new customer, to speed up your registration we reduced number of data fields to few from many.

Sign-in:Are you a Printoonite? You can sign-in freely and enjoy being a printoonite. As a Printoonite you can have following facilities.

  • Manage your Profile using My Profile.
  • Manage Your Favorite Merchandise using My Favorite Merchandise.
  • Manage Your Creative Designs using My Creativity.
  • Manage Your Graphics using My Graphics.
  • Manage Your Orders using My Orders.

    • My Profile: You can fill-in your profile with brief details about yourself, accept email, all are optional data fields. My Profile provides you facility to maintain regular updated Profile, Billing address in case of credit-card holder, Shipping address, Change password and much more.

    My Favorite Merchandise: Whenever you are browsing through Merchandise Catalogue you can add Merchandise to your basket i.e. My Favorite Merchandise. You can manage this basket with facility of categorizing your favorite merchandise and remove unwanted Merchandise from the basket.

    My Creativity: Whenever you create any design using Design Studio creaming your creativity, you can maintain and manage you valuable and creative design. Any time you can redesign your creativity and store them in basket i.e. My Creativity.

    My Graphics: You may be using your own graphics during creativity experience; you can shelf those graphics in My Graphics.

    My Orders: You will be having your creativity on one hand and your favorite Merchandise on the other and you would like to buy the Merchandise with your creativity inked on it, you can place the Order to Printoon. To manage those orders you can use My Orders.
